Conduct a Brake Stand Test

  • Apply park brake.
  • Set loco to Lead - Cut in.
  • Recover start up penalty if applied. Apply Independent, release Automatic Brake.
  • Check gauges (brake cyl approx. 560kpa, BP & ER approx. 500kpa, MR 750/850kpa, Flow 0.)
  • Release Independent Brake (do not depress, BC drops to 0)
  • Apply Automatic Brake to the minimum service position. (BP & ER approx. 450kpa. BC rises to approx. 130kpa)
  • Bail off the Independent Brake. (BC goes to 0 in approx. 10 secs.)
  • Cut out Automatic Brake only and monitor ‘flow’ and BP for 1 minute (BP must not drop more than 21kpa in 1 minute)
  • Cut in Automatic Brake. Apply to the full-service position. (Note BP drop and BC rise)
  • Slightly open the drivers and co/drivers emergency cocks in turn. BP drops to approx. 330kpa and flow rate is at approx. 1000. (Tests maintaining feature and ensures emergency cocks are unobstructed.) Close emergency cock.
  • Move the Automatic Brake handle to Emergency. (BP & ER = 0, BC =350kpa, initiates sanding, PCS open) Go to release after recovery countdown.
  • Ensure Independent and Automatic Brakes are fully released. Wait for VC to initiate an Emergency application. Recover Emergency application. Go to release after recovery countdown.
  • Apply Automatic Brake to full service. Place reverser into forward position, Gen field switch on. Move Dyn Brake handle to ‘setup’ then notch slowly through to at least notch 4. (Observe DB setup and notch indications in loco monitor. Note: Engine revs in 5000cl but not in 5020/6000cl beyond setup, BC reduces to 0.)
  • Apply Independent Brake. (BC rises). Release Independent Brake. (Do not depress). (BC pressure 0kpa.) Set Dynamic Brake to setup (with auto remaining in full service, BC pressure will rise to 350kpa.) 16. Go out of Dynamic. Centre reverser. (BC pressure 350kpa) 17. Apply Independent Brake, release the Automatic Brake (BP & ER = 500kpa, BC = 560kpa)
